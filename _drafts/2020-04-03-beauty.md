beauty is  
the thrill of  
a beating heart,  
cupped in a hand,  
with the pressures  
spelling potential,  
and its blood-rush  
lubdubs sounding the  
anthem of adrenaline  
as the eyes and mind  
imagine what it is  
to love and be loved back.
