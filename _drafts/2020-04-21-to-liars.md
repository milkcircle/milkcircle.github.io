i do not envy  
the need  
to build your kingdom  
from broken men,  
the compulsion to hide  
its bones beneath  
thick coats of paint,  
the falsehood of  
undeserved victory,  
the knowledge that  
you stand upon  
illegitimate ruins,  
revered for a narrative  
that masks disgrace.
