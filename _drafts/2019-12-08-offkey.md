you're off key  
and i'm off tempo  
and when you and i sing  
we're a little jazz misstep  
clashing like overtones:  
a little lopsided,  
like intoxicated imbalance,  
reaching to each other  
laughing  
as we stumble over our words and  
our toes  
sinking into the harmony of quiet
