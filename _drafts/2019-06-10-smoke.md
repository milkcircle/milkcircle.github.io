ephemeral smoke lingers  
an olfactory memory palace  
of ground tobacco dust  

it cannot be grasped  
it cannot be seen  
it cannot be felt  

it can only be tasted  
and occasionally sensed  
like a gray nostalgic past  

one that you know killed you.
