she is young and  
i watch her navigate  
privilege and goodness  
as closely as though  
she were family,  

i feel her tears  
like they are mine,  
and suffer the hole  
torn through her,  
emotional shrapnel  

yanking her down to reality  
with her eyes set  
to the clouds
