blue  
is the color  
of things turned cold,  
bloodrush absent,  
love untold --  

blue  
is the shade  
of love decayed --  
when time and space  
make bonds fade  

blue  
is the hue  
of i love you  
when it cracks and wilts 
as we bid adieu
