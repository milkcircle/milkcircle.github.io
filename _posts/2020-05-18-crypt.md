when the  
pace of the world  
quickens like sand  
between fingers, and  
county smog  
obfuscates the road ahead,  
i crawl where critters walk,  
lie my head against the  
earthen headrest,  
and remember that  
the pace of life  
is measured in breaths,  
memories, and smiling sighs.
