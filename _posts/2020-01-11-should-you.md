i've never seen an animal with a grudge.  
a dog will whimper,  
yelp,  
limp, with fur  
matted in blood,  
shivering under the  
whip of its  
beloved man  

and, moaning -- draw its last breath  
at the feet of its ward  

before it feels  
any everlasting hatred.
